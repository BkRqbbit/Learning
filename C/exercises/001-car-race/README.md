The staff of a car race, wants to calculate the maximum time the 12 participants need to finish the required laps.

## Info
You must read the number of the participant, and the time it taked to finish.\
Once you calculated what is the maximum time made, you must show both the time, and participant's number.

Time must be handled in seconds!

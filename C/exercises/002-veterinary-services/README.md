# Veterinary services
A veterinary wants to have a program that helps to determine the total amount that the client needs to pay, based on the services they choose for their pets.

1. bath ($3,500)
2. hair cut ($6,000)
3. vaccination ($12,500)
4. Bath and hair cut
5. Bath, hair cut, and vaccination
6. Bath and vaccination
7. Hair cut and vaccination

Depending on the user input, the program must show the total amount to pay.
